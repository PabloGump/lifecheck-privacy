1 Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use LifeCheck, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the rights you have regarding your data. By downloading or using LifeCheck, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

4 HealthKit Data

shield
Apple HealthKit — strict data protections apply

LifeCheck reads health data from Apple HealthKit solely to display your personal health summary within the app. HealthKit data is never shared with any third party, is never uploaded to any server, and is never used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to providing health features directly to you.

Specifically, regarding Apple HealthKit data:

  • Read-only access. LifeCheck requests permission to read — never to write — health data types including step count, workout sessions, sleep analysis, and heart rate.
  • Stays on your device. Health data read from HealthKit is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted to Firebase, RevenueCat, Expo, or any other service.
  • Never used for advertising. HealthKit data will not be used for advertising, marketing, or advertising networks of any kind.
  • Never sold. HealthKit data is never sold or disclosed for any commercial purpose.
  • Permission is optional. You may deny or revoke HealthKit access at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → LifeCheck. Core app features (tasks, habits, goals) remain fully functional without health access.
  • Compliance with Apple guidelines. Our use of HealthKit data complies with Apple's HealthKit framework requirements and the App Store Review Guidelines.

6 Data Retention & Deletion

historyHow long we retain your data

We retain your account data and user-generated content for as long as your account is active. Push notification tokens are refreshed and may be updated by iOS periodically. Usage and crash data (if collected) is retained according to Firebase's default retention periods, typically 14 months.

Local preferences stored via AsyncStorage remain on your device until you delete the app or clear app data in iOS Settings. This data is never uploaded and is unaffected by account deletion.

delete_foreverDeleting your account & data

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data. You can request this by:

  • In-app deletion (if available): Navigate to Settings → Account → Delete Account within LifeCheck.
  • Email request: Send an account deletion request to gaakcardona@icloud.com from the email address associated with your account.

Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will delete your account, email address, all user-generated content (tasks, habits, goals, notes, life events), social connections, messages, and push notification token from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as preventing fraud).

Please note: deleting your account within the app does not cancel an active subscription. To manage or cancel your subscription, go to iPhone Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions.

publicGDPR (European Economic Area, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or equivalent legislation. Our legal basis for processing your personal data is:

You may exercise your GDPR rights by contacting us at gaakcardona@icloud.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

flagCCPA (California residents)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grant you additional rights, including:

  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information — we do not sell or share personal information, so no opt-out is needed.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at gaakcardona@icloud.com. We will verify your identity before fulfilling a request and respond within 45 days as required by California law.
